Our Vision

  1. To be an organisation from, by, and for Indonesian students in NUS as well as to our nation.
  2. To create a kinship and mutual cooperation within the Indonesian students in NUS.
  3. To grow a leadership, proactive and responsible spirit within our members, and to maximize their potential in all field, so as to be able to give back to our nation in the future.

Our Mission

  1. To be a channel through which Indonesian students in NUS can create everlasting friendship and strengthen ties with each other.
  2. To be a student network center to assist our members in adapting to the NUS and Singapore environments.
  3. To be a channel through which Indonesian students in NUS develop their talents and cultivate leadership spirit.
  4. To be a channel through which Indonesian students in NUS develop their talents and cultivate leadership spirit.
  5. To carry out social, cultural and educational activities tailored for Indonesian students and/or non-Indonesians without political, racial, ethnic and religious associations.
  6. To be an ambassador and representation of Indonesian students in NUS.

About PINUS

PINUS (Perhimpunan Indonesia NUS or NUS Indonesian Community) is a group of Indonesian Students in National University of Singapore (NUS) established in 1998. Starting as a humble group of Indonesians created to facilitate simple gatherings, PINUS has now grown into one of the most established Indonesian student groups in Singapore. Currently, we have more than five hundred members, and the number is still growing steadily every year.
